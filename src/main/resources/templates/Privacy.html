Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Smart Parking is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information while you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Vehicle-related details provided during booking
  • Parking reservation details (date, time, location)
  • Login and usage information

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To process parking reservations
  • To send confirmations and notifications
  • To improve system performance

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with authorized parking partners and secure payment gateways when required.

5. Data Security

Appropriate security measures are implemented to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Cookies

Cookies are used to maintain user sessions and analyze platform performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. User Rights

Users may request access to or correction of their personal information by contacting our support team.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@smartparking.com.
